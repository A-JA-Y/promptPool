Meet the Creator

The mind behind PromptPool

Built with passion, shipped with purpose.

👨‍💻
Alex Mercer
Founder & CEO, PromptPool

Alex Mercer is a product engineer and AI enthusiast with over a decade of experience building developer tools and data-driven platforms. After spending five years leading engineering teams at early-stage startups in San Francisco and Bangalore, Alex noticed a recurring frustration: every team was reinventing the wheel when it came to crafting effective AI prompts. Developers, writers, and analysts were all searching the same forums, copy-pasting the same half-broken prompts, and wasting hours tweaking them to work. That frustration became the spark behind PromptPool—an open-source, community-curated prompt library designed to save time and elevate output quality. Alex holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from Stanford University and has contributed to several open-source NLP projects. When not building PromptPool, Alex enjoys trail running in the Himalayas, experimenting with sourdough recipes, and mentoring first-time founders through local incubator programs. Alex believes that the best tools are the ones that get out of your way and let you focus on what matters.
